💡 MONEY TIPS FOR TURKEY
💳 Cards: Visa/Mastercard widely accepted in Izmir & resorts. Notify your bank before travel.
🏧 ATMs: Use bank ATMs (Ziraat, Garanti, İşbank) — avoid private exchange kiosks at airports.
💵 Cash: Lira (TRY) is useful for markets, taxis, local cafes. EUR accepted in tourist areas.
📊 Rate tip: Exchange rates in Turkey are generally better than at UK airports.
⚠️ Avoid: Dynamic Currency Conversion (DCC) at card terminals — always pay in TRY.
